Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Portfolio Generator

We built Portfolio Generator to be as privacy-friendly as possible. We collect only what's necessary to run the service, we don't sell your data, and you can delete everything at any time.

Who We Are

Portfolio Generator ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered service that turns your GitHub profile into a professional portfolio website. We are operated independently and can be reached at edumatesupport@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • GitHub OAuth data — your GitHub username, display name, email address, and avatar. This is provided by GitHub when you sign in and is used to personalise your portfolio.
  • Public GitHub repository data — names, descriptions, stars, languages, and topics of your public repos. We only access public data; we never read private repositories.
  • Portfolio content — the bio, skills, and project descriptions you create or edit in the editor.
  • Subscription information — your subscription plan and expiry date. We do not store credit card or payment details; payments are handled manually via third-party transfer.
  • Telegram ID — if you link your Telegram account, we store your Telegram user ID to identify you in our payment bot.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as which pages you visit, used solely to diagnose errors.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and display your portfolio.
  • To authenticate you via GitHub OAuth.
  • To manage your subscription status.
  • To send you your activation code via Telegram (if applicable).
  • To improve the service and fix bugs.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting. Your data is stored on Supabase servers. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • GitHub API — to fetch your public repository data. GitHub Privacy Statement →
  • Groq / Llama 4 — AI model used to generate portfolio descriptions. Only your public GitHub data is sent to Groq. Groq Privacy Policy →
  • Vercel — website hosting. Vercel Privacy Policy →
  • Telegram — used by our payment bot to deliver activation codes. Telegram Privacy Policy →

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your portfolio or request account deletion, we will remove your personal data from our systems within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at edumatesupport@gmail.com.

Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required for authentication (via Supabase). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

Children's Privacy

Portfolio Generator is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
